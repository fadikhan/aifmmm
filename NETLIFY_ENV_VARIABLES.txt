========================================
HOW TO ADD IN NETLIFY DASHBOARD:
========================================

OPTION 1: During Initial Setup
-------------------------------
1. When importing your site, click "Show advanced"
2. Click "New variable" for each one above
3. Enter Key and Value
4. Click "Deploy site"

OPTION 2: After Site is Created
--------------------------------
1. Go to your site dashboard
2. Click "Site settings"
3. Click "Environment variables" in sidebar
4. Click "Add a variable"
5. Select "Add a single variable"
6. Enter Key and Value
7. Click "Create variable"
8. Repeat for all 6 variables
9. Go to "Deploys" and click "Trigger deploy"
